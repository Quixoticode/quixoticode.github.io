Add QSounds to Your Home Screen

Want to access QSounds faster, just like a regular app? Follow these simple steps to add QSounds to your Home Screen.

  1. Open the QSounds website in your Safari browser.
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the menu bar.
    (It's the icon with a square and an arrow pointing upwards: )
  3. Scroll down in the menu that opens.
  4. Tap on the option "Add to Home Screen".
  5. You can customize the name for the icon on your Home Screen (it defaults to "QSounds"). Then, tap "Add" in the top right corner.
  6. QSounds will now appear as an icon on your Home Screen!

Note for iOS: This feature works most reliably in the Safari browser. Other browsers on iOS may not offer this option directly.
